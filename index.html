Demon Slayer

Revolving areound feudal japan, Tanjiro embarks on a journey as a demon slayer with the objective of saving his sister, Nezuko. Later on, two more demon slayers, namely Zenitsu and Inosuke joins the force to complete the trio. Now, they are on their mission to save humanity from the demons.

My Hero Academia

Who doesn't want to become a hero right? How nice it would have been if there was an academy to train heroes. Well, Bnha revolves around this concept only. Midoriya who was born quirkless, i.e without any special power, inherited "One for All" from All might, the most powerfull hero there. And here starts his journey of becoming a pro hero along with his mates in the most prestigious academy.

Hunter X Hunter

Gon, a kid from the Whale Islands decides to follow on his father's footsteps and become a hunter. During the Hunter test he met Kurapika, Leoreo and Killua, and they formed a group immediately for the rest of the Hunter's exam. After the Hunter test the real journey began as Leoreo chased his dreams of becoming a doctor, Kurapika trained hard to take his revenge on the phantom troupe,
Gon went on to chase his father with Killua by his side. A story filled with beliefs, adventures and friendship, it is a must watch.
